Pancakes

Makes about 12 pancakes
10 Min - Prep time
20 Min - Cook time
30 Min - Total
4 Servings

INGREDIENTS

  • 1 1/2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 3 1/2 teaspoons baking powder
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1 tablespoon white sugar
  • 1 1/4 cups milk
  • 1 egg
  • 3 tablespoons butter, melted

DIRECTIONS

  1. Preheat Skillet: Heat a lightly oiled griddle or frying pan over medium-high heat.
  2. Mix Dry Ingredients: In a large bowl, sift together the flour, baking powder, salt, and sugar.
  3. Add Wet Ingredients: Make a well in the center and pour in the milk, egg, and melted butter; mix until smooth.
  4. Cook Pancakes: Pour or scoop the batter onto the griddle, using approximately 1/4 cup for each pancake. Cook until bubbles form on the surface, then flip and cook until browned on the other side.
